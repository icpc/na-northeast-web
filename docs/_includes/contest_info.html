{{ site.data.contest.year }} Contest Information

This year our contest will be on Saturday, February 25, 2023. We are currently finalizing the sites at which the contest will take place; we do not expect to use micro-sites this year unless there are exceptional circumstances.

The contest itself will be run through Kattis. We will provide a practice session in the week prior to the regional competition, in which teams can test both Kattis and our Zoom connection. Please ensure that you register with accurate email addresses so that we can contact you with the relevant details when the time comes.

Contest schedule:

  • 1:30 PM EST - Opening ceremony begins
  • 2:00 PM EST - Contest begins
  • 6:00 PM EST - Scoreboard freezes
  • 7:00 PM EST - Contest finishes
  • Shortly after - Closing ceremony/results

There WILL be nominal registration fees this year, exact amount TBD. We have two registration deadlines below, with the following meanings:

Feb 3 (first deadline): If you register by this date, you'll get ICPC t-shirts delivered prior to the contest. (Teams that register later will still get their t-shirts but they may arrive after the contest date. The t-shirts will be shipped to the university. Each team gets a free t-shirt for each of their (up to) three contestants, and the coach. Additional t-shirts can be ordered separately, a link will be sent by email.)

Feb 17 (second deadline): Registration deadline to ensure proper Kattis contest setup for your team(s) (we might be able to add your team(s) after the deadline - please contact us asap if you missed the deadline).
