{{ site.data.contest.year }} Contest Information

This year our contest will be on Saturday, March 5, 2022, starting (tentatively) at 2 PM EST. We will hold the contest using "micro-sites" in which each university will proctor their own teams (unless in a specific case one university offers to host teams from another university). Each micro-site must be proctored by a faculty member or other approved proctor and connected to a central Zoom call. Each team will have up to three competitors using a single computer, in the usual way. There is no limit to the number of teams per university, the more the merrier!

The contest itself will be run through Kattis. We will provide a practice session in the week prior to the regional competition, in which teams can test both Kattis and our Zoom connection. Please ensure that you register with accurate email addresses so that we can contact you with the relevant details when the time comes.

There are NO FEES this year. We do list two registration deadlines below, with the following meanings:

Feb 10 (first deadline): If you register by this date, you'll get ICPC t-shirts delivered prior to the contest. (Teams that register later will still get their t-shirts but they may arrive after the contest date. The t-shirts will be shipped to the university. Each team gets a free t-shirt for each of their (up to) three contestants, and the coach. Additional t-shirts can be ordered separately, a link will be sent by email.)

Feb 27 (second deadline): Registration deadline to ensure proper Kattis contest setup for your team(s) (we might be able to add your team(s) after the deadline - please contact us asap if you missed the deadline).
